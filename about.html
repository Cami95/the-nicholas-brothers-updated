They grew up in Philadelphia and were the sons of two musicians who played in their own band at the now permanetly closed, The Standard Theater, which was located at 1124 South St, Philadelphia, PA 19147. The theatre was also refered to as "The Old Standard Theater".Their mother played piano while their father played the drums. From a young age, the brothers where exposed to alot of Vaudeville acts but they specificly showed interest in the dancers. These acts included notable talents such as Alice Whitman , Willie Bryant and Bill Bobinson .Even with this being the case, they never had an formal dance training.
