Where did these talented brothers come from?

  • Fayard Antonio Nicholas was born in Mobile, Alabama on October 20th, 1914
  • Harlod Lloyd Nicholas was born in Winston-Salem, Norht Carolina on March 17th, 1921

They grew up in Philadelphia and were the sons of two musicians who played in their own band at the old standard theater. Their mother played piano while their father played the drums. From a young age, the brothers where exposed to alot of Vaudeville acts but they specificly showed interest in the dancers. These acts included notable talents such as Alice Whitman, Willie Bryant and Bill Robinson.Even with this being the case, they never had an formal dance training.
