The Nickolas Brothers

Who were they?

The Nicholas Brothers were a famous African American duo or dancing brothers, Fayard and Harlod Nickolas, who climbed the ladder to global and internationa fame during the 1930s and the 1950s. With their highly acrobatic technique, high level of artistry and daring innovations, they were considered the greatest tap dancers of their day.
